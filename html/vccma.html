Variable Capital Companies (Miscellaneous Amendments) Act

Overview

The Variable Capital Companies (Miscellaneous Amendments) Act (No. 28 of 2019) amends the Goods and Services Tax Act, the Income Tax Act, and the Stamp Duties Act in connection with variable capital companies and their sub‑funds, amends the Variable Capital Companies Act 2018 in connection with the enactment of the Insolvency, Restructuring and Dissolution Act 2018, and makes related amendments to certain other Acts.

The Act became law on 23 September 2019. Certain sections of the Act became effective on 15 January 2020. The remainder of the Act will become effective on the date the Minister appoints by notification in the Gazette.

Regulator: Monetary Authority of Singapore (Prime Minister’s Office)
