Commodity Trading Act 1992

Overview

The Commodity Trading Act 1992 regulates spot commodity trading. The Securities and Futures Act regulates other types of commodity trading.

The Act became effective on 27 May 1992.

Regulator: Enterprise Singapore Board (Ministry of Trade and Industry)
