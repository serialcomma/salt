Intellectual Property (Dispute Resolution) Act 2019

Overview

The Intellectual Property (Dispute Resolution) Act 2019 (No. 23 of 2019) clarifies that intellectual property disputes can be arbitrated, improves processes to enhance the quality of issued patents, and consolidates most civil IP proceedings in the High Court.

The Act became law on 29 August 2019. Parts 1, 4, sections 5, 9, 12, 20(b), (e), and (i), 21, 22(c) and (d), 26, 29, 33, and Part 10 became effective on 21 November 2019. Sections 6, 10, 11, 22(a), 23, 24(a), 28, and 34 became effective on 1 October 2021

Regulator: Ministry of Law
