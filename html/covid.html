COVID-19 (Temporary Measures) Act 2020

Overview

The COVID-19 (Temporary Measures) Act 2020 (No. 14 of 2020) provides temporary measures, and deals with other matters, relating to the COVID‑19 pandemic.

The Act became effective on 8 April 2020, with certain sections effective on other dates.

Regulator: Ministry of Health
