Personal Data Protection Act 2012

Overview

The Personal Data Protection Act 2012 (No. 26 of 2012) governs the collection, use and disclosure of personal data by organisations and establishes the Do Not Call Register.

The Act became effective on various dates from 2 January 2013 to 2 July 2014.

Regulator: Personal Data Protection Commission (Ministry of Communications and Information)
