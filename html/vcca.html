Variable Capital Companies Act 2018

Overview

The Variable Capital Companies Act 2018 (No. 44 of 2018) creates a new type of corporate entity, the variable capital company, and provides the legislative framework to govern them. Variable capital companies exclusively support collective investment schemes.

The Act became effective on 14 January 2020.

Regulator: Monetary Authority of Singapore (Prime Minister’s Office)

There are uncommenced Amendments to this Act.
