Payment Services Act 2019

Overview

The Payment Services Act 2019 (No. 2 of 2019) provides for the licensing and regulation of payment service providers and the oversight of payment systems.

The Act became effective on 28 January 2020 (Parts 1 to 8, sections 109, 110, 112, 115 to 120, Part 10, and the First and Second Schedules). Section 114 became effective on 30 July 2020.

The Payment Services (Amendment) Act 2021 is not yet effective. It will become effective on the date appointed by notice in the Gazette.

Regulator: Monetary Authority of Singapore (Prime Minister’s Office)
