Securities and Futures Act

Overview

The Securities and Futures Act (Chapter 289) regulates the securities and derivatives industry.

The Act became effective on various dates from 1 January 2002 to 1 October 2002.

Regulator: Monetary Authority of Singapore (Prime Minister’s Office)
