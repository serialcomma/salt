Precious Stones and Precious Metals (Prevention of Money Laundering and Terrorism Financing) Act 2019

Overview

The Precious Stones and Precious Metals (Prevention of Money Laundering and Terrorism Financing) Act 2019 (No. 7 of 2019) regulates certain dealers of precious stones and metals and their intermediaries to prevent money laundering and terrorism financing.

The Act became effective on 10 April 2019.

Regulator: Ministry of Law
